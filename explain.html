Convenience

GT sports steering wheel.

A distinct motorsport sensibility: The uniquely designed spoke trim as well as the GT Sport steering wheel with a thumbrest and 12 o'clock mark clearly demonstrates the 911's motorsport DNA. The decorative ring around the Porsche Crest is in black and the gearshift paddles are in dark silver. The GT Sport steering wheel is finished in leather with cross-stitched seams. Race-Tex®¹ trims are available upon request. ¹Race-Tex is a race-track-approved microfibre material with a high-quality look and feel, similar to suede. It has a comfortable, soft grip and offers good hold. It is durable and easy to care for.

Adaptive Sports Seat Plus

The Adaptive Sport Seat Plus (18-way) is standard on the 911 Turbo S model. The stiff, sporty padding is placed on the side bolsters for even greater lateral support. It also includes a memory function for all seat positions in the driver's seat (except for side bolsters). Headrest has ' the Turbo ' or ' the Turbo has turned the S' logo, the center of the seat back is quilted applies.

Rear seat

The 911 has rear seats, providing superior comfort compared to a typical sports car. Like the front seats, the center of the seat back is quilted. The shelves at the back of the rear seats can be used as additional storage space. Plus, thanks to the foldable backrest, the 911 Turbo Coupe models give you plenty of space for your suitcase. On the 911 Turbo Cabriolet model, this loadspace has a capacity of 163 liters.
